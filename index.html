About Us

ASH Civic Partners is a philanthropic initiative offering free Community Economic Impact Reports for small businesses and startups. We believe in the power of data and storytelling to help communities thrive.

Our Services

We create personalized Community Economic Impact Reports to help businesses:

  • Understand their local economic footprint
  • Strengthen grant and loan applications
  • Communicate their value to stakeholders and the public
